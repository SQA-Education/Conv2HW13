3 · Write Asking to Swap a Shift

Write a short dialogue between you and a co-worker. You need your co-worker to swap shifts with you. Use at least two vocabulary words and at least one conditional.

💬 Second conditional for polite requests:
"If you covered my Saturday, I would take your Sunday."
"It would be a win-win if we traded shifts."

💬 Try to use: touch base, negotiate, compromise, feasible, win-win situation.

  • Write it as a dialogue (you + a co-worker)
  • Ask your co-worker to swap/trade shifts
  • At least 2 lesson vocabulary words
  • At least 1 conditional (If…, would…)
